Motivation Mate - AI Desktop Companion
========================================

Boost your productivity with a cute AI companion!

**Motivation Mate** is a desktop mascot application that monitors your activity and keeps you focused on your goals. Powered by Google's Gemini AI, it "sees" what you are working on and provides personalized encouragement (or gentle scolding!) to help you stay productive.

### How to Use

1.  Launch `MotivationMate.exe`.
2.  Click the mascot to open **Settings**.
3.  Enter your **Gemini API Key** (Free from Google AI Studio).
4.  Set your **Current Mode** (e.g., "Writing a report").
5.  Let Motivation Mate do the rest!

### Requirements

*   Windows 10/11
*   Internet Connection (for AI analysis)
*   Google Gemini API Key
